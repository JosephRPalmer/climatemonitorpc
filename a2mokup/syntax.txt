SYNTAX FOR TCP COMMS 

SYSTEM MESSAGES
ALWAYS START WITH SYSMM

EMAIL STARTS WITH EADD1 OR EADD2

PHONE NUMBER STARTS WITH PADD1 OR PADD2

MESSAGES TO THE PC SYSTEM

STARTS WITH SR2DB TO SEND RECORD 2 DATABASE

STARTS WITH DISPL TO DISPLAY MESSAGE

SAMPLE FROM CCM = 
SR2DB 01/01/0001 02:00:00 AM 4 5 6
